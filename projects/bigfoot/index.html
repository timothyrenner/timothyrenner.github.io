Finding Bigfoot


The Bigfoot Field Researchers Organization (BFRO) maintains records of verified Bigfoot sightings throughout North America. A group in Pennsylvania has a bounty of one million dollars for information leading to the capture of a sasquatch.

I like data. I like money. The next step is obvious: use my technical and scientific skills to help find this thing.

I pulled the data from the BFRO website and have a sample of it hosted on https://data.world/.

  • Data Extraction Code: https://github.com/timothyrenner/bfro_sightings_data
  • Dataset: https://data.world/timothyrenner/bfro-sightings-data
